About

Dominique Schmidt was raised Edgewater, Florida and currently resides in DeLand. With a growing interest in graphic design during high school, she currently studies Digital Arts and minors in Marketing at Stetson University. During her studies, she has been the Digital Media Intern at Collective Church, Communications Assistant at Stetson University Athletics, and most recently promoted to Head Layout Designer for the Stetson Reporter Magazine. Moving into her final year of undergrad, Dominique is working towards creating her own approach to digital marketing, and moving on to pursue a masters in integrative digital living. In her free time she enjoys drinking coffee, spending time with her three cats, browsing the internet, and getting a good night's sleep.
